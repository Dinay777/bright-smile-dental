About Us

Caring for Houston Families Since 2011

At Bright Smile Dental, we believe everyone deserves access to quality dental care in a comfortable, welcoming environment. Our team is led by Dr. Elena Petrova, DDS, who brings over 15 years of experience in general and cosmetic dentistry.

Our modern Houston office is equipped with digital X-rays, intraoral cameras, and the latest dental technology to ensure precise, comfortable treatments.

  • Multilingual team: English, Spanish, Russian
  • Modern, state-of-the-art equipment
  • Gentle approach — ideal for anxious patients
  • Family-friendly — we treat patients of all ages
  • Convenient location with free parking
